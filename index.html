Single player 2D maze and multiple levels based on

AI Vaccum

A representative way of displaying AI search algorithms
processes with a 2D grid and a vaccum trying to clean
each dirty room in the enviornment. Showcases BFS, UCS,
A*, and DFS in Python. Displays each search algorithms
observable states of the enviornment.
